About Feed Big Tex

Feed Big Tex! is a matching game honoring my Texas roots with a State Fair of Texas theme. At the beginning of each round, a random number of fair visitors generates. The player must make matches to equal the number of fair visitors to win the game.

Art Break is exactly what it sounds like - a way to use art to take a break from the world. Art Break is a fully functional React app that receives live data from the Harvard Art Museums API, displays the data randomly, and allows you to draw on a digital sketchpad.
